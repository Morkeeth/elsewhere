# Elsewhere — 2:45 demo script

## 0:00–0:20 — The problem

“Big life decisions are not questions with answers. They are futures with different failure modes. Asking one AI for advice collapses those differences into one confident paragraph.”

Show the question: **Should I stay in Paris, move to London, go remote, or build a studio?** Open the decision studio briefly to establish that the inputs and shock are editable.

## 0:20–0:55 — Four futures

Press **Open the futures**.

“Elsewhere computes four twelve-month world states from cited salaries, tax rules, rent, costs, and explicit personal assumptions. Every number is formula-owned. At the same time, four GPT-5.6 witnesses inhabit the same ledgers through different lenses: money, belonging, reversibility, and failure.”

Point to annual net, fixed monthly cost, year-end savings, optionality, and the vertical door-narrows marker. Open one witness note.

## 0:55–1:35 — Break the worlds

Toggle the parent-care shock.

“A decision that only works in the expected world is not a robust decision. In month six, a parent needs recurring care and travel. Elsewhere reruns every future. Watch the same lives pull apart.”

Point to the red shock line and divergence change. Emphasize that shock timing, cost, travel, energy, and belonging effects are editable.

## 1:35–2:05 — The exit

Scroll into the acid-green experiment.

“The synthesis is not allowed to choose. It finds the uncertainty worth buying down. Here: borrow the most uncertain future for fourteen days, cap the money at risk, and collect three observable signals.”

Read the first physical step, not the whole hypothesis.

## 2:05–2:30 — Evidence and architecture

Open **Evidence**.

“This is the trust boundary: 100% trace coverage, dated public sources, visible formulas. GPT can interpret this ledger; it cannot edit it. Four Responses run concurrently and a fifth preserves their disagreement in synthesis.”

## 2:30–2:45 — Close

Show **Export brief**, **World states**, and **Send to OpenClaw**.

“Elsewhere turns a private, anxious decision into an inspectable set of futures, a stress test, and one real action. Don’t ask AI what to choose. Visit the futures first.”

## Recording checklist

- Use a desktop viewport around 1440×900.
- Begin with the page already loaded and the API key configured.
- Keep the evidence drawer closed until the architecture reveal.
- Do not wait silently during witness execution; narrate the deterministic ledger appearing immediately.
- Capture the engine status showing four witnesses plus one synthesis.
- Stay below three minutes.
